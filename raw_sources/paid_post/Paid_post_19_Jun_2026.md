Whatever RE I own, I am quite happy. And, do NOT wish to sell it.

In fact, some of the best investments (RE) I made were in Goa. And, these have done exceptionally well.

Takeway: irrespective of the portfolio size.

1) Own 50-60% (at least) assets for growth (especially if you are young).
2) If you can't own RE, own gold (physical). This is for wealth protection.
3) Diversify as much as possible (eg. if you own ONLY India, own US; & vice-versa).

I'm transparent, you guys can ask me anything.
