# Principle Fragility Report

*Assessing the "Blast Radius" if a hardcoded principle is factually wrong or mathematically incomplete.*

### PRIN-001: Follow Government Subsidies
*   **What happens if wrong?** The system will output "Buy" recommendations for heavily subsidized, but fundamentally unviable industries (e.g., Solar panel manufacturing pre-2020 where Chinese dumping destroyed margins despite subsidies).
*   **System function?** Yes, but it will lose money.
*   **Future phases dependent:** Sector Mapping, Trend Templates.
*   **Blast Radius:** **Critical** (Government policy triggers are the primary tailwinds in the MVP).

### PRIN-002: Avoid Capital-Intensive Execution Traps
*   **What happens if wrong?** The system will categorically reject the entire Infrastructure, Energy, and Semiconductor sectors, missing massive bull cycles.
*   **System function?** Yes, but it generates massive False Negatives.
*   **Future phases dependent:** Stock Selection (Veto logic).
*   **Blast Radius:** **Critical** (It is the only generic constraint in the system and triggers on almost every test).

### PRIN-003: Follow Geopolitical Supply-Chain Shifts
*   **What happens if wrong?** Misinterprets temporary political posturing (e.g., a single tariff) as a multi-decade structural shift, leading to premature capital deployment.
*   **System function?** Yes.
*   **Future phases dependent:** Framework Generation.
*   **Blast Radius:** **Medium**.

### PRIN-004: Avoid State-Owned Enterprises (PSUs)
*   **What happens if wrong?** The system will completely miss Government-mandated re-rating cycles (e.g., Indian Defense PSUs 2021-2024 or Railway PSUs).
*   **System function?** Yes.
*   **Future phases dependent:** Stock Veto Filter.
*   **Blast Radius:** **High** (Blinds the system to the largest market cap sector in India during specific sovereign cycles).

### PRIN-005: Follow Monopsony Buyers
*   **What happens if wrong?** Traps capital in companies with massive order books but negative cash flow (because the single buyer dictates payment terms and crushes margins).
*   **System function?** Yes.
*   **Future phases dependent:** Sector Selection.
*   **Blast Radius:** **High**.

### PRIN-006: Follow Demographic Destiny
*   **What happens if wrong?** Traps capital in "story stocks." Demographics take 50 years to play out; markets price in 1 year. The principle provides zero timing edge.
*   **System function?** Yes.
*   **Future phases dependent:** Trend Templates.
*   **Blast Radius:** **Medium**.

### PRIN-007: Avoid Sunset / ESG Restricted Industries
*   **What happens if wrong?** Misses the "Last Man Standing" cash-flow bonanza (e.g., Coal/Thermal power in 2022-2023 when no new supply came online but demand spiked).
*   **System function?** Yes.
*   **Future phases dependent:** Stock Veto Filter.
*   **Blast Radius:** **Medium**.
