# Principle Taxonomy Review

*To prevent duplicate principles, synonym fragmentation, and flat hierarchies, the Principle Catalog must enforce a strict Parent → Child → Tactic/Constraint taxonomy.*

## Final Recommended Taxonomy

### 1. Macro-Structural Variables (The Parents)
*These are the highest-level, slowest-moving forces.*
*   **[PARENT] Demographic Destiny**
    *   *Child:* Aging Populations (Healthcare/Wealth Management)
    *   *Child:* Youth Bulge (Consumption/Financialization)
*   **[PARENT] Capital Flow & Liquidity**
    *   *Child:* Global Interest Rate Cycles (Cost of Capital)
    *   *Child:* Sovereign/FDI Routing (Geopolitical De-risking / China+1)
*   **[PARENT] State Intervention**
    *   *Child:* Government Subsidies/Incentives (PLI/CHIPS Act)
    *   *Child:* Regulatory Monopolies/Mandates (Defense Indigenization)

### 2. Micro-Economic Execution (The Children)
*How businesses capture the Macro-Structural variables.*
*   **[CHILD] The Monopoly/Oligopoly Premium**
    *   *Tactic:* Prefer consolidated markets (e.g., Telecom)
    *   *Tactic:* Prefer Picks & Shovels in a gold rush
*   **[CHILD] Operating Leverage**
    *   *Constraint:* Avoid high variable-cost businesses during inflation.
    *   *Tactic:* Seek margin expansion via formalization (stealing unorganized market share).
*   **[CHILD] Capital Efficiency (ROCE)**
    *   *Constraint:* Avoid capital-heavy execution traps without a state mandate.
    *   *Constraint:* Avoid State-Owned Enterprises (PSUs) outside of strict Monopsony environments.

### 3. Valuation & Risk Discipline (The Constraints)
*The immutable laws that override all Tailwinds.*
*   **[CONSTRAINT] Price-to-Earnings Disconnect**
    *   *Rule:* If Price CAGR vastly exceeds Earnings CAGR over 3 years, downgrade confidence.
    *   *Rule:* Do not pay peak multiples for cyclical earnings.
*   **[CONSTRAINT] Portfolio Survival**
    *   *Rule:* Max 10% allocation per equity thesis.
    *   *Rule:* Retain 20% liquid/hard-asset dry powder.

---

## How this prevents fragmentation:
1.  **No Synonyms:** "Follow FII Money," "Track Liquidity," and "Watch US Rates" all collapse into the **`[PARENT] Capital Flow & Liquidity`** node.
2.  **Resolved Collisions:** A `[CHILD]` tailwind (e.g., Operating Leverage) can be mathematically vetoed by a `[CONSTRAINT]` (e.g., Price-to-Earnings Disconnect).
3.  **Inheritance:** If a Macro Event triggers `State Intervention`, the system automatically inherits the context to check for `Capital Efficiency (ROCE)` traps.
