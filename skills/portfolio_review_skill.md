# Skill: Portfolio Review

*Layer: L1 — Core Skills*

## Purpose
Audit a portfolio for liquidity, diversification, concentration, barbell balance, and actionable improvement.

## Execution Steps

### Step 1: Liquidity Audit
- Total liquid % (cash + equities + MFs + easily redeemable assets)
- Target: 60–80% liquid
- Flag if: liquid < 60%

### Step 2: Barbell Check
- Left side (preservation): Cash, Gold, liquid instruments — what % of total?
- Right side (growth): Equities, tech, AI exposure — what % of total?
- Middle (mediocre): Fixed deposits, long-duration bonds, underperforming real estate — flag these

### Step 3: Geographic Diversification
- India-only? → FLAG (single-country risk)
- India + US? → Acceptable base
- India + US + 1 emerging market? → Good
- No global exposure? → FLAG and recommend US equity or index ETF

### Step 4: Concentration Risk
- Any single position > 10% of portfolio? → FLAG
- Any single sector > 30% of equity? → FLAG
- Any illiquid position > 20% of net worth? → FLAG

### Step 5: Dry Powder
- Cash + liquid assets available for next correction?
- If < 5% of portfolio is deployable cash → FLAG (insufficient dry powder)
- Identify overheated positions where partial profit harvest makes sense

### Step 6: Missing Asset Classes
- Physical gold or equivalent hard-asset hedge? If NO → recommend 5–10%
- International equity exposure? If NO → recommend minimum 10–20%

## Output Format
1. Overall Health Score: Strong / Adequate / Needs Work / At Risk
2. Allocation Table (Current vs Recommended)
3. Red Flags List (numbered)
4. Recommended Adjustments (prioritized)
5. Next Actions (specific, actionable)
