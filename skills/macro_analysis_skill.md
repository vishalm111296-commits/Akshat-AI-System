# Skill: Macro Analysis

*Layer: L1 — Core Skills*

## Purpose
Assess global macro environment to frame all investment decisions. Called as a prerequisite before most equity analysis tasks.

## Execution Steps

### Step 1: Global Liquidity Assessment
- Is central bank liquidity expanding (QE, rate cuts, balance sheet growth)?
- Is liquidity tightening (rate hikes, QT)?
- What is the next directional move most likely to be?

### Step 2: Interest Rate Direction
- Current policy rate trajectory
- Bond market signals (10Y yield direction)
- Probability of pivot (cut/hike) in next 6 months

### Step 3: Currency & INR Analysis (for India-related queries)
- INR vs USD trend (12-month)
- Current account deficit status
- FII flow direction (net inflows / outflows over 3 months)

### Step 4: Drawdown Classification
- Is the current market move:
  - **V-shaped panic**: sudden drop, sentiment-driven, fundamentals intact → buying opportunity
  - **W-shaped recovery**: tentative, repeated retests → cautious accumulation
  - **Structural damage**: earnings impairment, credit crisis, policy failure → preserve capital

### Step 5: AI Cycle Monetization Check
- Is the AI infrastructure buildout still accelerating?
- Are hyperscalers (MSFT, AMZN, GOOGL, META) still guiding capex growth?
- Is software/cybersecurity/healthcare layer beginning to monetize?

## Output
- Macro Environment: [Favorable / Neutral / Unfavorable]
- Liquidity: [Expanding / Tightening / Neutral]
- Rate Direction: [Hiking / Cutting / Pausing]
- India Risk: [High / Medium / Low]
- Recommended Posture: [Aggressive / Neutral / Defensive]
